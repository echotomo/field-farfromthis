When I visit Museums, I often take photos and write notes about the artists I find most inspiring. 
These photos essentially languish in my phone, and I'll occasionally revisit them if I'm able to search for a specific artist.
The objective of artistcards is to start collecting these artists and their works as if they were baseball cards. 

On this MVP page will essentially be a photo gallery of cards (images of the artwork on one side, metadata about the art and artist on the back), but the gallery is a stack of cards. 
Each card contains the image of the piece, artist name, artist metadata, artist statement or notes, my personal notes. One side of the card is just the image of the piece, the other side of the card contains the metadata.
