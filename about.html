About

Field

Field.farfromthis is the archive of the artwork and artists that inspire me. I often take business cards and photos from the museums, gallery openings, and craft fairs I attend, but I then let the collected documentation languish on my device, or scattered across my studio shelves.

Field is an attempt to archive this ephemera, and share what I witnessed with others.

Far From This

Far From This is an online visual journal. This publication collects a series of illustrated works exploring the interactions of people, the spaces they occupy, and the objects they create. It is a synthesis of the content already made available across social media platforms and communities.

Far From This has always served as a creative metronome for the editor, maintaining a continuous creative rhythm. Over the course of its evolution, this publication advocates for those not yet certain how to express the hidden worlds they occupy, and to demonstrate that we can find our voice through practice.
